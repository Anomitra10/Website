Add Ons To Music Files

Music has multiple formats such as WAV, AAC, MP3, DSD etc.
But did you know Music can be listened in many ways such as 3D, 8D, 12D, Bass Boosted etc?
Here is a Table of a Variety of Addons for music files.

Audio Variant Effect App used for It
3D Focuses on replicating real-world spatial sound perception using binaural or spatial audio techniques. FL Studio
8D Uses panning effects to create the illusion of sound moving around the listener, primarily for a dynamic listening experience. FL Studio
12D An enhanced version of 8D audio with more complex sound manipulation to create an even more immersive effect. FL Studio
Reverb Adds an Echo Effect to the Audio making you Feel like your in a Tunnel. FL Studio
